Requirements & Test Day

Here's what you need to know before booking your theory test.

Valid Omang

Bring your valid Omang or passport for identification at the test centre.

Test Fee

Pay the required DRTS theory test booking fee at the time of booking.

Booking Date

Confirm your test date and arrive at least 30 minutes before your scheduled time.

Booking Receipt

Bring your booking or payment receipt if issued by DRTS.

Study First

Prepare with Road Wise Academy before booking your test.

After Passing

Apply for your learner's licence and start practical driving lessons.
